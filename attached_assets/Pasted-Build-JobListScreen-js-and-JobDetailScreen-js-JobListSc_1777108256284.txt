--- JobListScreen.js ---
A scrollable list of all collector jobs (pending, accepted, completed).

Layout:
- White header bar with title "My Jobs" (18pt, fontWeight 700). No back button —
  this is a tab screen.
- Three horizontally scrollable filter tabs below the header: "All", "Pending",
  "Accepted", "Completed". Active tab has a green underline and dark text.
  Inactive tabs have muted grey text. Implemented with a FlatList horizontal.
- Job cards (JobCard.js component) in a vertical FlatList. Each card:
  - White background, borderRadius 12, marginHorizontal 16, marginVertical 6,
    elevation 2, padding 14.
  - Left: a coloured square icon representing waste type (plastic=blue, metal=grey,
    paper=yellow, glass=teal), 44x44, borderRadius 10.
  - Centre: Household area (14pt, fontWeight 600), waste type + weight on one line
    (13pt, #6B7280), time posted ("Posted 12 min ago", 12pt, #9CA3AF).
  - Right: StatusBadge component (small pill: green for pending, amber for accepted,
    grey for completed) and a chevron "›".
  - Tapping the card navigates to JobDetailScreen with the job object.

--- JobDetailScreen.js ---
Full details of one pickup job.

- Header bar with back arrow and title "Job Details".

    Waste Type | Weight Estimate
    Area       | Distance
    Posted     | Status badge

  "Bags are by the gate").
- A green "Accept This Job" button (full width, 52px tall, borderRadius 24).
